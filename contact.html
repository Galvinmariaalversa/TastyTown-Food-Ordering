Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my live order status?
Once your order is placed, you will be redirected to the success page. Click "Track Order" to view the live status tracker showing preparation, dispatch, and delivery progress in real-time.
Do you offer catering for corporate events?
Yes, we do! TastyTown handles catering orders of any scale. Simply select "Catering & Corporate Orders" in the form drop-down, or email us at events@tastytown.com with your custom menu requirements.
Can I modify my delivery address after placing an order?
If your order has not been dispatched yet, you can modify your address by calling our assigned rider directly or contacting customer support immediately via our hotline at +1 234 567 8900.
What is the TastyTown hygiene guarantee?
We enforce a zero-tolerance hygiene policy. All partner kitchens are inspected monthly, kitchen crews wear sanitized gloves and masks, and food is packed in temper-proof, insulated thermal pouches.
